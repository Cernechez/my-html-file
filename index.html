STUDENT INFORMATION RECORD (SIR)

The primary purpose of a student information record (SIR) is to collect, organize, and manage all student-related data, facilitating efficient administration, communication, and tracking of academic progress and other relevant information.

DATA PRIVACY STATEMENT. In strict compliance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012, please be assured that all your basic information will be treated with utmost confidentiality. It is also assured that it will not be shared with any third party and will only be used for the Guidance Office's student database and profiling.
